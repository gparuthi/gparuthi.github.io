Artificially Intelligent Grid Assistant (A.I.G.A)

Published 20 Feb 2008.

AIGA is a Grid based application which works in collaboration with natural language processing (NLP), to act as a virtual teacher. The application can answer queries in a conversational manner and is capable of being deployed in various scenarios. We also created a working model of our system, deploying various existing frameworks as our sub-systems.
